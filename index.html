5. Daily Maintenance & Care

Maintenance recommendations
  • It is suggested to re-connect the hardware each week to refresh the hardware connection ports.
  • The hardware and cables are not waterproof. Do not pour liquids directly into the hardware.
  • The accessory wiring harness is a vulnerable component. It is recommended to replace one every three months to ensure stability, it is not suggested to use tape or glue to secure the CCB, it may influence the heat dissipation.

6. Troubleshooting / FAQ

6.1 Connection Failures

⚠️ Interface shows "Please connect hardware first" (White Text)
  1. Data cable reversed: Ensure the cable is connected to the end with the car icon on the CCB.
  2. CCB not powered: Check if the data cable is plugged in correctly and if the CCB light is on green, and if screen frequently display the notification, please try replacing the data cable.
  3. CCB malfunction: Try replacing the CCB to verify.
⚠️ Interface shows "Please connect hardware first" (Red Text)
  1. Unplug and replug the CCB.
  2. Keep the CCB plugged in, click Settings (bottom right), scroll to the bottom and click "Restore Default Settings". Try multiple times.
  3. Restart the car head unit.
  4. CCB firmware is too old: Contact the dealer to upgrade the firmware.
CCB Bluetooth cannot connect
  1. Unplug and replug the CCB.
  2. On your phone, "Forget" the CCB's Bluetooth name.
  3. Reconnect the CCB Bluetooth.
iPhone shows "Accessory not supported" when connecting Bluetooth
  1. Try replacing the data cable.
  2. Restart the phone.

Note: If the above methods fail, please contact after-sales service.

Stuck on "Connecting" after Bluetooth connection
  1. Delete the CCB's Bluetooth and WiFi connections from phone and reconnect.
  2. For iPhone: Go to Phone Settings > General > CarPlay, delete the record, restart the phone, and reconnect.
  3. For Android phone: Go to Play Store > Android Auto > Delete it and re-install Android Auto app > Restart the phone.
Android phone can connect, but iPhone cannot connect

Check iPhone's side:

  1. Please check both of your Wi-Fi and Bluetooth remains open, and disconnect any devices that are already connected.
  2. Make sure your iPhone's personal hotspot is OFF.
  3. Please go to Settings > General > Carplay > My Car, make sure the device you recognized is ON.
  4. Change an iPhone to test.

Check CCB's side:

  1. Please re-plug the dongle, and try connection.
  2. Use your phone to forget dongle's bluetooth, and reconnect.
  3. Restart the vehicle.

6.2 Unexpected Disconnections

⚠️ Automatic disconnection in good signal areas
  1. Unplug and replug the CCB, then reconnect.
  2. Keep the CCB plugged in, click Settings (bottom right), scroll to bottom and click "Restore Default Settings", then try reconnecting.
  3. "Forget" the CCB Bluetooth from phone, turn off and on phone WiFi and Bluetooth, then reconnect.
  4. For iPhone: Go to Phone Settings > General > CarPlay, delete the record, restart the phone, and reconnect.
  5. For Android phone: Go to Play Store > Android Auto > Delete it and re-install Android Auto app > Restart the phone.
  6. Go to App Settings (bottom right) > Advanced Settings > Wireless Settings, change to a different WiFi frequency band.

Note: If the above methods fail, please contact after-sales service.

Disconnection on bumpy roads or when touching the CCB
  1. Check if the data cable is plugged in securely.
  2. Try replacing the data cable.

Note: If disconnections remain frequent, please contact after-sales service.

Disconnection in tunnels, mountains, or poor signal areas
  1. This is normal, reconnect after leaving the place.
  2. Refresh phone's cellular data.
Disconnection after making a phone call
  1. Unplug and replug the CCB and restart the phone.
  2. Keep the CCB plugged in, click Settings (bottom right), scroll to bottom and click "Restore Default Settings", then try reconnecting.

Note: If disconnections remain frequent, please contact after-sales service.

6.3 Installation Failure

⚠️ ADB Installation
  1. If the ADB address is not obtained, check with following lists:
    1. Computer should have Android Debug Bridge (ADB) environment, if not, please use installation package provided by importer.
    2. Vehicle's ADB mode should be ON.
    3. For company's computer with restrictions, please contact the IT department to unlock USB debugging mode.
    4. Change cable.
  2. If the ADB failed on installing software:
    1. Check CCB's software version, make sure using correct software package for vehicle.

Note: If unsolved, please contact after-sales service.

⚠️ Flash Drive Installation
  1. If flash drive installation failed:
    1. Check software's name, make sure the name is com.bz.CA08.Adapter.apk
    2. Check flash drive's type, only specific flash drive provided by importer can be used for installation, normal flash drive won't work.
    3. Update vehicle's IVI to the latest version.

Note: If unsolved, please contact after-sales service.

6.4 Screen Frozen

  1. Unplug and replug the CCB.
  2. Restart the phone.

6.5 Poor Quality of Sound

  1. Go to app's setting > Advanced settings > Change audio channel to bluetooth.
  2. Use phone to connect both vehicle's bluetooth and CCB's bluetooth.

Note: If unsolved, please contact after-sales service.

6.6 Music/Audio Problems

⚠️ Music/Audio unexpected stop
  1. Disconnect phone's bluetooth connection with vehicle.
  2. Go to App Settings (bottom right) > Advanced Settings > Wireless Settings, change to a higher WiFi frequency band.
Music/audio comes from phone not vehicle
  1. Switch channel to CarPlay/Android Auto from phone's Music/Audio settings.
  2. Disconnect phone's bluetooth connection with vehicle.
Music/audio delay
  1. Go to phone's website > Media delay > set to 500.
  2. Replug the CCB.
Music/audio notification doesn't pop up
  1. Go to phone's permission > change permission to 'Allow only while using the app'.
  2. Restart the phone.

6.7 GPS positioning is inaccurate

  1. Go to phone's Debug page > Settings > Turn off GPS.
  2. Restart the phone.

7.2 Installation Guide

Note: The following installation download links only provide CCB software. For specific installation steps and tutorials, please consult your importer to obtain the download links.

  1. This vehicle requires a SIM card installation and 5G connection (ensure mobile data is available, not Wi-Fi).
  2. Lock the car and leave it for 10 minutes (power off to allow software download).
  3. Return to the vehicle and power it on (software installation).
  4. The "Clock" APP should appear on the desktop:
  5. Plug in the external CCB box.
  6. Return to the Head Unit screen, open the Clock app, then return to the Head Unit screen. The CarConnect APP should now appear:
  7. Open the Car Connect APP that appeared.
  8. In the Car Connect interface, follow the on-screen instructions to connect your phone's Bluetooth to the box, then you can start CarPlay.

Note: When you see the Clock APP added to your apps, the installation is successful. If the installation is not successful, first ensure network connectivity, then power off - wait ten minutes - power on and try again.
